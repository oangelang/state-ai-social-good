For social impact practitioners
New to AI?
You do the work. You don't need to understand transformers — you need to know what AI can realistically do in your domain, what has failed, and how to evaluate vendor claims. Start with the SSIR landscape and the AI Now annual report.

For AI practitioners
New to social impact?
You understand the models. You need to understand the context: why deployment fails, what "community" means in practice, who the funders are, and how the social sector actually makes decisions. Start with Fast Forward's AI for Humanity report.
Recommended: Fast Forward → DataKind → domain pages → Ecosystem map
