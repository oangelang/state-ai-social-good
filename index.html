The real risks

Full risk analysis →

A site about AI for social good that doesn't name the harms is a brochure, not a field resource. These six categories are documented, active, and inadequately addressed.

01
Bias & Discrimination
AI systems trained on historical data inherit and amplify existing inequalities. Hiring tools, credit scoring, medical diagnostics, and criminal justice have all produced documented discriminatory outcomes even after explicit debiasing.
55% of AI experts and 55% of the US public are highly worried about AI bias — the rare issue they agree on.Pew Research, 2025
02
Epistemic Risk
Deepfakes, synthetic media, and AI misinformation erode the shared factual basis of public life. AI impersonations scammed people out of $5.3 billion in 2025.
70% of AI experts are highly worried about AI-generated inaccurate information — the top shared concern across groups.Pew Research, 2025
03
Environmental Cost
AI's carbon and water footprint is growing faster than most climate commitments. Training GPT-3 evaporated 700,000 liters of freshwater. US data centers projected at 12% of national electricity by 2028.
AI carbon footprint: 32.6–79.7M tons CO₂ in 2025. Water: up to 764 billion liters.ScienceDirect, 2025
04
Economic Disruption
Job displacement is uneven and accelerating. Entry-level roles are declining fastest — the exact roles that build career foundations.
56% of US adults are very concerned about AI eliminating jobs. Only 25% of AI experts share that concern.Pew Research, 2025
05
Security & Warfare
AI is lowering the barrier for cyberattacks and autonomous weapons. Biometric data from humanitarian crises has been misused by state actors in multiple documented cases.
AI deepfake-as-a-service financial fraud caused billions in losses in 2025.AIhub, 2026
06
Governance & Power
AI capability is concentrating in a handful of companies and governments. The EU AI Act enforcement begins August 2026. The US still has no federal AI law.
45 US states proposed AI bills in 2024. 31 enacted laws. Zero at the federal level.Cimplifi, 2025
